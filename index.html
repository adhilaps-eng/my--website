Home Baker Turns Entrepreneur

Lady Loafella, as the name signifies, is a women-owned enterprise. It is a reflection of strong dedication and passion towards baking without compromising quality and integrity.

Each bake from our oven is a product of love and labour, handcrafted and served with the smallest details taken care of.

From the quality of the ingredients to the aesthetics, every item on the menu will take you to different parts of the globe.
